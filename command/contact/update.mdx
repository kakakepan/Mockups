The **"Update Contact"** command is used to update the data of a contact related to a domain.

# Accessing the Update Contact Command

To access the **"Update Contact"** command, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the SuperReseller dashboard [here](https://superreseller-qa.nominus.host/).
2. Select the **"Online Form"** menu.
3. Click on **"Update Contact"** under the **"Contact"** section.
4. On the **"Modify Contact"** page, you will find a form to update the contact data. 

<Warning>
**Caution:**
- Ensure to provide the required information as per the restrictions and guidelines specified for the command.
- Providing correct and authentic information will maintain data integrity and ensure accurate updates.
</Warning>
