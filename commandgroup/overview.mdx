**Superreseller's Command groups** feature revolutionizes the way users manage domains and contacts within the dashboard. By categorizing commands into groups such as **"Domains"** and **"Contact"**, Superreseller simplifies the process of locating and executing domain-related operations. This structured approach enhances efficiency, improves the user experience, and enables an organized workflow, resulting in effective domain and contact management within the Superreseller ecosystem.
