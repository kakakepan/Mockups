The **"Account"** section provides access to the reseller information and allows users to view and edit their account details.  This section grants you the flexibility to make necessary modifications to your account.

## Access the Account section

1. Click on the name of the user in the top right corner of the screen.
2. From the displayed options, click on the **"Account"** link.
3. The Account page will be displayed, showing the reseller information for the logged-in user.

## Reseller Information

The **"Reseller Information"** section displays the details of the reseller's account. These fields are read-only and cannot be edited. The information includes:

| Field                           | Description                                                                                                               |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Reseller ID                     | The unique identifier of the reseller.                                                                                    |
| First Name                      | The given or first name of the individual.                                                                                |
| Last Name                       | The surname or family name of the individual.                                                                             |
| Organization Name               | The name of the organization or company.                                                                                  |
| Main Email                      | The primary email address for general communication.                                                                      |
| Main Urgent Email               | An email address designated for urgent or critical communications.                                                        |
| Address                         | The street address or physical location of the contact.                                                                   |
| City                            | The city or locality of the contact’s address.                                                                            |

## Editing Account Details

To edit the account details, follow the steps below:

1. On the homepage, click the **“Profile”** option on the top-right side of your screen.
2. Click **“Account”** option to proceed.
3. On the Account page, click the **"Edit"** button.
4. The editable fields will be enabled for modification.

<Note>
The fields under the **"Reseller Information"** section cannot be edited.
</Note>

5. Click the **"Save"** button to save the changes made.

<Warning>
**Caution:**
Please exercise caution when editing the account details and ensure that the information entered is both accurate and up-to-date.
</Warning>
