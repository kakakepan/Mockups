Within the **"Agreements"** option, the **"TLDs (Top-Level Domains) Terms"**  are specific rules and conditions set by the registry or governing body for a TLD. These terms cover domain registration, ownership, and administration, including eligibility criteria, naming restrictions, dispute resolution guidelines, and obligations of registrants. They also address renewal, expiration, restoration procedures, and associated fees. It is crucial for clients to understand and follow the TLDs Terms to avoid suspension or cancellation of their registrations, making informed decisions during the registration and management process.

# TLDs Terms

To view the **"TLDs (Top-Level Domains) Terms"** for your country specific domain, website, or online service, please follow the steps outlined below:

1. Click on the "**TLDs (Top-Level Domains) Terms"** feature under the **"Agreements**" option, which provides information such as the following:

| Agreement   | Description                                                                                                           |
| :---------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Extension   | The command or action that initiates a specific event or operation related to the TLDs (Top-Level Domains) operation. |
| Last Update | Outlining the last date of update for domain services within the TLD.                                                 |
| Terms       | Specifies the terms related to TLDs extension.                                                                        |
